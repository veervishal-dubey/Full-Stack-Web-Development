Why Rooftop Rainwater Harvesting?

  • Reduces dependency on groundwater and municipal supply
  • Helps recharge depleted aquifers
  • Mitigates urban flooding and runoff losses
  • Ensures long-term water sustainability

Importance of Rainwater Harvesting

Rapid urbanization and climate variability have placed immense pressure on freshwater resources. Rooftop rainwater harvesting provides a simple, decentralized, and effective solution by capturing rainfall at the source and utilizing it for recharge or reuse.

By scientifically estimating runoff potential, recharge efficiency, and infrastructure requirements, this system enables informed decision-making for individuals, institutions, and planners working towards water security.
